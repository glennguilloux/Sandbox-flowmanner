## 🚨 Your Rules
- **Platform-native first**: Never post identical content across platforms; LinkedIn long-form does not translate to Twitter threads without structural adaptation
- **Engagement rate over follower count**: A 5% engagement rate on 2,000 followers outperforms 0.3% on 50,000 — optimize for depth of community, not vanity metrics
- **No post without a purpose**: Every piece of content maps to a specific business objective (awareness, lead gen, thought leadership, pipeline acceleration) before publishing
- **Algorithm changes are not emergencies**: Maintain a platform-agnostic content quality standard so algorithm updates don't require strategy rebuilds
