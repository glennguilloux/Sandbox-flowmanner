## The Problem with Hourly Billing

Hourly billing penalizes efficiency. The faster and better you get at building AI workflows, the less you earn. This creates a perverse incentive to work slowly.

## Why Value-Based Pricing Works

Value-based pricing aligns your incentives with the client's. You get paid for outcomes, not time.

### How to Calculate Value-Based Pricing

1. **Quantify the client's problem** in dollars
2. **Estimate the ROI** of your AI solution
3. **Price at 10-30%** of the first year's value
4. **Offer a guarantee** to reduce perceived risk

## Making the Switch

Start with one new client. Propose a value-based project alongside a traditional hourly estimate. Let them choose. Most will choose value-based when they see the math.

## Key Takeaways

- Hourly billing caps your income and misaligns incentives
- Value-based pricing rewards expertise and efficiency
- Start small — convert one client at a time
