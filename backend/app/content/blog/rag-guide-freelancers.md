## What Is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation gives AI models access to external knowledge without retraining.

## How RAG Works

### 1. Index Documents

Upload documents to a vector database.

### 2. Retrieve Context

Search for relevant chunks using semantic similarity.

### 3. Generate with Context

The AI receives both the question and relevant documents.

## Best Practices

- Chunk size: 500-1000 tokens works best
- Tag documents with metadata
- Keep knowledge base fresh
