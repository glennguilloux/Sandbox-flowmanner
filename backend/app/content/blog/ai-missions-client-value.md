## Why Most AI Workflows Fail Clients

The biggest mistake AI freelancers make is starting with the tool instead of the outcome.

## The Mission-First Framework

At FlowManner, we use a three-step framework:

### 1. Discovery

Before writing code, define what success means in business terms.

### 2. Plan

Map the steps to get there. Break work into discrete tasks.

### 3. Execute and Iterate

Run the mission, review results, and iterate.

## Key Takeaways

- Always start with the client business outcome
- Use mission planning to map steps before building
- Build in feedback loops for continuous improvement
