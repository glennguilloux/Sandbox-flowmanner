## Open questions

1. **Pydantic v2 forward-ref + Decimal fallbacks** — chunk 3 had a
   forward-ref bug (`from __future__ import annotations` + Pydantic v2
   didn't resolve).  Chunk 5's followup (49dc90f) had a similar issue
   with `Decimal('0')` violating `gt=0`.  Both were caught by
   regression tests added AFTER the original commit.  Consider adding
   a project lint rule: "test models with NULL/empty inputs" as part
   of the test-driven-development loop.  Out of scope for chunks 4-5.
2. **HANDOFF_LEASE_LOST emission coverage** — currently only emitted
   from `accept_with_packet` when renew fails.  The lease reclaimer
   (chunk 1) can also steal a lease mid-handoff.  Should the
   reclaimer also call into HandoffProtocol to emit the event?
   Probably yes, but the cross-module call surface is bigger.
   Defer to a future hardening pass.
3. **`HandoffLeaseIntegration.transfer()` worker_id is instance-bound**
   — even with the new `new_worker_id` param, you still need to know
   the target worker_id at the call site.  In production, the "new
   worker" is whatever consumer picks up the handoff next, which
   may not be known at transfer time.  This may need a more
   sophisticated design (lease-token pattern, or the new worker
   just claims with its own worker_id when it starts processing).
   Out of scope for chunk 5.
